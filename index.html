Open When...

Open when you miss me

Even if we are far from each other, my heart is always reaching for you. If you miss me, read this and remember that distance cannot erase what I feel. I carry you in my prayers, in my quiet moments, and in every plan I make for the future. I may not be beside you right now, but my love still chooses you every day. When the night feels long, think of this: I am under the same sky, thinking of your smile, and wishing I could hold your hand. You are never alone in this, not for one second.

Open when you are tired

Rest, breathe, and be gentle with yourself. You do not need to be perfect today to be worthy of love. I admire your strength, but I also love your softness and your need to pause. It is okay to slow down, to cry, to sleep, and to leave some things unfinished for tomorrow. You have carried so much, and I am proud of you even on your quiet, heavy days. If I were there, I would remind you to drink water, breathe deeply, and let your heart rest. You are doing better than you think, and I believe in you so much.

Open when you doubt us

I choose you with patience and intention. Distance may test us, but my feelings are sincere and steady, not temporary and not shallow. I know we are still growing through this season, and I know some days feel uncertain, but I will keep showing up with honesty, effort, and consistency. I do not want a rushed love. I want a real one that survives hard days, silence, misunderstandings, and miles between us. If doubt visits your mind, come back to this: I am here, I am serious, and I am still choosing us one day at a time.

My heart is safest when it is with you.

No matter how many miles are between us, I will keep loving you with patience, respect, and steady intention.

No matter where life brings us, I will always be grateful that I met you. You are the kind of love I prayed for and the peace I did not know I needed.
